# Design Direction

## Typography
- Font: Poppins (display + body)
- Hierarchy: bold headings, medium labels, regular body

## Colors
- Primary: zinc-900 (sidebar), zinc-50 (bg)
- Accent: blue-600 (#2563eb)
- Success: emerald-600
- Danger: red-600
- Warning: amber-600
- Text: zinc-800, zinc-500 for secondary

## Layout
- Sidebar nav (desktop), bottom tabs (mobile)
- Stat cards grid at top
- Data table center
- Chart on right/bottom
- Modals for actions

## Style
- Clean, professional, minimal
- White cards with subtle borders
- Monospace for currency amounts
- Uppercase tiny labels
- Blue tags for case categories
